Our Services

At S.M. Interior Decor, we provide comprehensive interior design and décor solutions that blend creativity, comfort, and durability. From modern homes to professional workspaces, our wide range of services is designed to enhance the beauty and functionality of every space. With expertise in wallpapers, PVC & WPC panels, flooring, false ceilings, modular kitchens, and custom décor, we deliver stylish and long-lasting results tailored to your unique vision.

Wallpaper Installation (2D/3D)

Give your walls a refreshing makeover with our exclusive 2D and 3D wallpaper collection. From elegant textures to bold designs, our wallpapers add depth, character, and a modern touch to any room.

PVC & WPC Panel Work

Upgrade your walls and ceilings with our durable PVC and WPC panel solutions. Resistant to water and termites, these panels not only provide strength but also elevate the look of your interiors with style and elegance.

WPC & PVC Board Installation

Perfect for partitions, furniture, and wall cladding, our WPC & PVC boards are eco-friendly, long-lasting, and moisture-resistant. Designed to combine strength with beauty, they are an excellent choice for modern interiors.

Flooring & Vinyl Solutions

We offer a wide range of flooring solutions including vinyl, wooden, and designer tiling. Our flooring options are stylish, durable, and easy to maintain, giving your space a premium finish and long-lasting appeal.

ACP (Aluminum Composite Panel) Work

Our ACP solutions are ideal for both interiors and exteriors, offering a modern look with durability. These lightweight, weather-resistant panels enhance aesthetics while ensuring structural strength and longevity.

False Ceiling Work

Transform your ceilings with innovative designs in POP, gypsum, and wooden false ceilings. Our solutions enhance lighting, improve insulation, and add a touch of luxury to your interiors.

Aluminum & Steel Fabrication

From sturdy doors and windows to modern partitions, our aluminum and steel fabrication work combines durability with sleek finishes. Every project is crafted to enhance functionality and elegance.

Electrical & Lighting Solutions

Lighting defines the mood of any space. We provide complete electrical and lighting setups, including modern LED installations and decorative lighting solutions, to brighten up your interiors beautifully.

Modular Kitchen & Bathroom Work

Upgrade your home with modular kitchens and bathrooms designed for efficiency and style. Our customized solutions ensure maximum space utilization with a sleek, modern look.

Smart Architecture & Layout Design

We create smart layouts and architecture plans that maximize space, functionality, and aesthetics. Our designs are carefully crafted to reflect modern living standards and client-specific needs.

Texture & Decorative Painting

Add life to your walls with our decorative and texture painting services. From classic finishes to trendy artistic patterns, we deliver painting solutions that redefine your interiors.

Interior Decoration & Art Work

Our interior decoration and artwork services bring creativity and uniqueness to every project. From wall art to customized décor pieces, we add the finishing touches that make your space truly special.
